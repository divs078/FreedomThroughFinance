Podcasts

If you don't listen to podcasts, it's about time you start now! The greatest advantage of listening to podcasts is that you can listen on the go! It is hands-free, eyes-free and makes you look forward to your morning commute and daily chores. In a world where we perpetually seem to be busier and busier trying to balance career and personal time, podcasts add a leisurely form of multitasking to your schedule. Here are some of Finance for Freedom recommendations.

BiggerPockets Money
Brown Ambition
ChooseFI
How To Money
Suze Orman's Women and Money

Books

If you are not the podcast-type, books are great to catch up on some great financial advise! Everything you read fills your head with new bits of information, and you never know when it might come in handy. The more financial knowledge you have, the better-equipped you are to tackle any challenge you’ll ever face. Here are some of Finance for Freedom recommendations.

The Total Money Makeover
Rich Dad Poor Dad
The Automatic Millionaire
Broke Millennial
The Millionaire Next Door
